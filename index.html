Connecting on iOS

  1. Force close Among Us completely.
  2. Tap the orange Connect button above.
  3. Tap Open when Safari prompts you.

Type these commands in lobby chat to set active gamemodes:

Standard Classic /gamemode classic

Standard Among Us gameplay with custom server settings and roles enabled.

Hide and Seek /gamemode hideandseek

Impostor is revealed instantly. Crewmates must complete tasks before being hunted down.

Shift Lock / Speedrun /gamemode shiftlock

Fast-paced mode with accelerated cooldowns, modified vision, and instant kills.

Type /roles in-game to view or configure roles:

Sheriff /role sheriff

Crewmate with a kill button. Killing an Impostor saves the crew, but killing a Crewmate eliminates the Sheriff!

Jester /role jester

Neutral role. Has no tasks and wins the game immediately if voted out during a meeting.

Medic /role medic

Crewmate that can shield a player from being killed or inspect dead bodies for clues.
