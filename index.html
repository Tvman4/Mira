Quick Instructions

  1. Force close Among Us completely.
  2. Tap the orange Connect button above.
  3. Tap Open when prompted.

Type these commands in lobby chat to change gamemodes:

Standard Classic /gamemode classic

Standard Among Us rules with custom roles and server commands enabled.

Zombies / Infection /gamemode zombies

Impostor infects crewmates on touch. Survived crewmates must complete tasks to win.

Jester Royale /gamemode jester

Every player tries to act as suspicious as possible. First person voted out wins!

Hide and Seek /gamemode hideandseek

Impostor is revealed at start. No meetings or voting—pure survival.

Murder Mystery /gamemode murder

1 Killer, 1 Detective with a gun, and innocent Crewmates holding tasks.

Prop Hunt /gamemode prophunt

Crewmates disguise as map items while Impostors search the map to find them.

Shift Lock Speedrun /gamemode shiftlock

Extreme player movement speeds with zero kill cooldowns and instant task fixes.

Death Run /gamemode deathrun

Impostor activates map hazards while Crewmates race to finish the obstacle course.

High Speed Tag /gamemode tag

Pass the tag bomb before the timer expires. Last standing player wins.

Town of Host /gamemode townofhost

Full modded ruleset with up to 15 unique active sub-roles running simultaneously.

Type /roles in-game to view active role configurations:

Sheriff /role sheriff

Crewmate with a weapon. Eliminating an Impostor saves the crew; targeting a Crewmate eliminates yourself.

Jester /role jester

Neutral role. Has no tasks and wins immediately if voted out during a meeting.

Medic /role medic

Can place a protective shield on one player or inspect dead bodies for death reports.

Engineer /role engineer

Crewmate that can access vents and remotely fix sabotages from anywhere on the map.

Mayor /role mayor

Crewmate leader whose vote counts as two during emergency meetings.

Swapper /role swapper

Can exchange the total votes between two players during emergency meetings.

Godfather /role godfather

Impostor leader who appears as a innocent Crewmate to Sheriff and Detective scans.

Morphling /role morphling

Impostor who can sample another player's appearance and transform into them temporarily.

Arsonist /role arsonist

Neutral role. Douses players in gasoline and ignites them all at once for an instant solo win.

Executioner /role executioner

Neutral role assigned a specific target. Wins if their target gets voted out.

Phantom /role phantom

Becomes completely invisible for a short duration after finishing a task.

Vulture /role vulture

Neutral role that must locate and eat a set number of bodies to achieve victory.
